Expand description

Constants to be used for set_video_mode and set_video_mode_sub. The DS has 2 rendering engines, Main and Sub, that can be put in different modes (6 modes and 5 modes respectively). Mode6_2d and the ones suffixed “3D” are only valid for Main (The 3D engine renders on Background 0). Modes FB0-FB3 (“LCD” mode) map the Banks A-D respectively to pixels on screen.
